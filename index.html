Each mode uses the Digital Crown differently. Spin to focus, reverse to calm, hold to breathe. Your wrist, your rhythm.

01
Guide Mode
Digital Crown · Follow the guide
A moving guide appears within the dial. Rotate the Digital Crown to follow it and watch the visual effects respond as you stay close. A mindfulness feature designed to anchor your attention without effort.
Tap the guide button to activate. Tap again to return to free spinning.
02
Stealth Mode
Digital Crown · Dim & discreet
Reduces the dial’s brightness for discreet fidgeting. Perfect for virtual meetings — keep your hands busy and your mind present without drawing attention. Ideal for winding down before sleep.
Tap the moon button to toggle between dim and bright.
03
Into the Stars
Digital Crown · Clockwise to accelerate · Anti-clockwise to reverse
Journey through space at your own pace. Spin clockwise to glide forward through the stars, building speed with each turn. Reverse by spinning anti-clockwise. A mindful moment of calm — and if you’re lucky, a rare comet will streak across your path.
The speed is entirely yours to control.
04
Weather the Storm
Digital Crown · Clockwise to intensify · Anti-clockwise to calm
Rotate clockwise to build from a soft drizzle to a powerful storm, complete with thunder and lightning. Spin back to find calm. A pulsating “Breathe” label guides your breathing as the rain falls — find your perfect rhythm for mindfulness and calm.
The intensity is yours. So is the calm.

The story

Built from a
personal need

It started with a sensation. The feel of the Digital Crown — the haptic feedback, the satisfying resistance of each turn. There was nothing on Apple Watch that simply let you spin it. No app built around that feeling.

So Crown Fidget was born. Not as a product, but as a personal tool — something to reach for instead of a phone. A way to stop doom scrolling. Something for the hands that didn’t demand attention from the mind.

It worked. And then it grew.

“Crown Fidget started as my own focus tool — something I could use discreetly when my mind needed steadying. Orbit Pong grew from the same instinct — the same Crown, the same haptics, now with a gentle challenge to keep your mind present.”
